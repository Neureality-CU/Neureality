Neureality

Hackathon

The first hackathon focusing on bridging neurotech and virtual reality.

The hackathon will run for three days, bringing together neuroscientists, computer scientists, designers, hackers, and enthusiasts. The goal is to explore what novel interactions neurotech and BCI can bring to virtual reality.
