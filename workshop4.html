Build XR App with Unity Platform

Date and Time:

Sunday, March3 , 11AM - 1 PM, EST

Duration:

2 hours

Workshop Overview:

Unity is a powerful 2D/3D/AR/VR game engine, I will give you a brief background information about Unity. After this tutorial, you will be able to set up your quest 2 device with Unity, create your first VR project, write your own code with some basic interactions in Unity, and upload your entire project to GitHub. It will be a three-step lecture, the first part will be some basic background information about Unity software. The second part will be showing you how to download and connect the app with your quest 2 device and create your first VR project with the proper settings. The third part will be a hands on activity to teach you how to write some simple code in Unity and make some basic interact with Unity.

Target Audience:

The primary audience will be the people who have no prior knowledge about Unity or Unity VR. Some coding background will be helpful (C#)

Preparation Requirements:

Please download Unity Hub and have 2022.3.10.f1 version installed on your Unity Hub. Also please download the Meta Quest mobile on your mobile device as we use it to connect with your quest 2 device during the hackathon event.
